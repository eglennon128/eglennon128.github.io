Welcome to my professional portfolio, where I showcase my expertise as a certified drone pilot and GIS specialist serving clients across Maryland. With a recent degree in Geographic Information Systems from the University of Maryland and Part 107 certification from the FAA, I combine advanced UAV operations with geospatial analysis to deliver precise, high-quality aerial solutions. My services include real estate photography and videography, infrastructure inspections, and GIS-integrated mapping, all tailored to meet the needs of industries like construction, agriculture, and environmental management. Whether you're a real estate agent seeking stunning property visuals or a developer needing detailed site surveys, I'm committed to exceeding expectations with reliable, efficient, and cost-effective results.

About Me

I am Ethan Glennon, a dedicated GIS professional and drone enthusiast based in Maryland, holding a bachelor's degree in Geographic Information Systems from the University of Maryland. My passion for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) stems from their transformative potential in data collection and analysis, which I honed through rigorous academic coursework and hands-on projects. As a Part 107 certified drone pilot, I specialize in operating advanced equipment like the DJI Mavic 4 to capture high-resolution imagery, which I then process using GIS tools such as ArcGIS and QGIS for creating orthomosaics, 3D models, and custom geospatial maps. Currently building my career through freelance side gigs and personal initiatives, I've gained practical experience in real-world applications, including property inspections and environmental monitoring. This blend of technical skills and innovative mindset drives me to advance UAV technology, ensuring accurate and actionable insights for clients in diverse sectors like real estate and infrastructure development. I look forward to collaborating on projects that push the boundaries of aerial and geospatial innovation.
